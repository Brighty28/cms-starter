⚙ THE REUSABILITY SYSTEM

Create one base Next.js + Sanity starter repo with shared components (nav, hero, sections, footer, contact form, CMS schema). Each template is a styled variant of the same engine. When an order comes in: git clone the template → swap content + theme → deploy to Vercel → invite client to Sanity Studio. Your per-order time drops to 4–8 hours, which is what makes the volume model actually profitable.

07 — LAUNCH SEQUENCE

Your First-Week Launch Steps

Do these in order. The goal of week one isn't profit — it's a live, polished gig with at least one template ready to show, so you can convert your very first buyer cleanly.

1

Build your base starter repo (1 weekend)

Spin up npx create-next-app + npm create sanity@latest. Build shared components: nav, hero, sections, contact form, footer. This is the engine every template clones from. Deploy a demo to Vercel.

2

Build 3 live demo templates (2–3 evenings)

Style the engine into Restaurant, Trades, and Clinic variants. Deploy each to its own Vercel subdomain (e.g. restaurant-demo.vercel.app). These become your gig gallery images and live proof.

3

Capture gig gallery assets (1 evening)

Screenshot each demo on desktop + mobile. Fiverr lets you upload 3 images + a video. Record a 60-sec Loom showing a template + editing content live in Sanity. Video gigs convert far better.

4

Create the Fiverr gig (1 evening)

Paste in your title, the three packages, description, tags, extras, and FAQ from this pack. Set launch pricing (~30% lower). Complete your seller profile with a real photo and a short, confident bio.

5

Drive your first orders (ongoing)

Fiverr's algorithm needs initial traction. Share your gig link on LinkedIn, in relevant subreddits (r/forhire), and to anyone you know who needs a site. The first 3 reviews are the hardest and most valuable — chase them hard.

6

Deliver, over-deliver, get the 5★ (per order)

Respond within hours, deliver early, include the Loom walkthrough even on cheaper tiers at first. A wall of 5★ reviews in month one sets up everything that follows. Then raise prices.

08 — PRO TIPS

Edge That Most Sellers Miss

Respond to messages in under 1 hour

Fiverr ranks gigs partly on response time. Set up the Fiverr app with notifications. Fast replies in the first 24h of an enquiry dramatically lift your conversion and ranking.

Always "message before ordering"

Encourage buyers to message first. It lets you scope properly, avoid mismatched orders (which risk bad reviews), and upsell the right package. Mismatched auto-orders are the #1 cause of disputes.

Your demos ARE your portfolio

Unlike most Fiverr sellers showing static mockups, your live Vercel demos let buyers click around real sites. This is a massive trust signal and almost no one at your price point does it.

Funnel premium leads off-platform (legally)

When a buyer needs something bigger (full Umbraco build, e-commerce, ongoing dev), you can complete it on Fiverr at a custom quote. Repeat clients who trust you become your premium pipeline.

Use Buyer Requests early

New sellers can browse Buyer Requests and send custom offers. In your first weeks, send 5–10 tailored offers per day. This is the fastest way to land that crucial first order before reviews exist.

Bank the Fiverr income separately

Route Fiverr payouts straight to your "Tesla Fund" / "Debt Nuke" pots from your main plan. Keep 20% aside for HMRC. Every clean order moves both goals forward.
