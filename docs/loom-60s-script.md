# 60-Second Loom Script — Fiverr Gig Video

A tight, benefit-led walkthrough to upload as your gig video. Video gigs convert
noticeably better than image-only gigs, and showing **live editing** is the one
thing almost no seller at your price point does.

**Before you record**
- Have two tabs open: the **live demo** (e.g. `your-demo.vercel.app/trades`) and the **Sanity Studio** for that demo.
- Close notifications, set your screen to a clean resolution, record at 1080p.
- Speak in a calm, confident, friendly tone — you're the expert, not a salesperson.
- Total target: **55–60 seconds.** Don't rush; cut dead air in editing instead.

---

## The Script (with on-screen actions)

**[0:00–0:08] — Hook + the promise** *(on screen: the live demo homepage, scroll slowly down the hero)*

> "Hi — I build modern, fast websites for small businesses, with one big difference: you can edit everything yourself. Let me show you the same site, live."

**[0:08–0:20] — Show the finished site** *(scroll through services, reviews, contact)*

> "This is a real, deployed site — fully responsive, loads instantly, and it's ready for Google. Services, reviews, contact — all clean and professional, on every device."

*(quickly drag the browser narrower to show it snap to mobile layout)*

> "And here it is on a phone — same site, automatically."

**[0:20–0:42] — The magic moment: edit live** *(switch to the Sanity Studio tab)*

> "Now here's the part that saves you money. This is your content manager. Say you want to change a price or some text…"

*(click a field — e.g. a service title or the hero tagline — type a small change, hit Publish)*

> "I just change it here… and publish."

*(switch back to the live site tab, refresh)*

> "Refresh the site — and there's the update. No developer, no waiting, no extra cost. You're in full control."

**[0:42–0:55] — Why you** *(back to the homepage, slow scroll)*

> "I've got 20-plus years of engineering experience, so your site is built properly — fast, secure and easy to maintain. You pick a template, send me your details, and I tailor it to your brand."

**[0:55–1:00] — Call to action** *(end on the hero or your gig title card)*

> "Message me before you order and I'll recommend the right package. Let's get your business online — properly."

---

## Tips

- **The edit-and-refresh moment is the whole video.** If you only nail one thing, nail that. Pick a visible change (the big hero headline or a price) so the before/after is obvious.
- Keep the cursor movements slow and deliberate — fast clicks read as nervous.
- Add captions in Loom/CapCut; ~85% of buyers skim with sound off at first.
- Export and also drop this same clip into your "WHAT YOU GET" gig image set.
- Re-record it per niche later if you like (a trades version, a restaurant version) — but one solid general version is plenty to launch.
