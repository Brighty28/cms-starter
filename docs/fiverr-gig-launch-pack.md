## 01 — Gig Title

Fiverr titles must start with "I will". Lead with the buyer's outcome, not your tech. Most buyers don't know what Sanity is — they know they want a fast, editable website. Keep tech as a credibility signal, not the headline.

| Title option | Note |
| --- | --- |
| **I will design a modern responsive website with an easy to edit CMS** | ★ LEAD WITH THIS |
| I will build a fast Next.js business website with a content manager | tech-aware buyers |
| I will create a professional website you can update yourself, no code | non-technical buyers |
| I will develop a blazing fast website with Sanity CMS and Next.js | developer clients |

**Why the top one wins:** "Easy to edit CMS" is your differentiator vs cheap static-HTML sellers, and "modern responsive" matches what buyers actually type into Fiverr search. It signals the benefit (you control your own content) without naming a CMS they've never heard of. Save "Sanity" and "Next.js" for the description and tags, where they boost search relevance for the buyers who do know them.

Your edge is reusability. Build each of these ONCE as a Sanity + Next.js starter, then clone-and-customise per order. This is what turns a 30-hour build into a 6-hour build. Start with the top 3 — they cover the highest-demand, lowest-complexity niches.

## 07 — Launch Sequence

Do these in order. The goal of week one isn't profit — it's a live, polished gig with at least one template ready to show, so you can convert your very first buyer cleanly.

1. **Build your base starter repo (1 weekend)** — Spin up `npx create-next-app` + `npm create sanity@latest`. Build shared components: nav, hero, sections, contact form, footer. This is the engine every template clones from. Deploy a demo to Vercel.
2. **Build 3 live demo templates (2–3 evenings)** — Style the engine into Restaurant, Trades, and Clinic variants. Deploy each to its own Vercel subdomain (e.g. restaurant-demo.vercel.app). These become your gig gallery images and live proof.
3. **Capture gig gallery assets (1 evening)** — Screenshot each demo on desktop + mobile. Fiverr lets you upload 3 images + a video. Record a 60-sec Loom showing a template + editing content live in Sanity. Video gigs convert far better.
4. **Create the Fiverr gig (1 evening)** — Paste in your title, the three packages, description, tags, extras, and FAQ from this pack. Set launch pricing (~30% lower). Complete your seller profile with a real photo and a short, confident bio.
5. **Drive your first orders (ongoing)** — Fiverr's algorithm needs initial traction. Share your gig link on LinkedIn, in relevant subreddits (r/forhire), and to anyone you know who needs a site. The first 3 reviews are the hardest and most valuable — chase them hard.
6. **Deliver, over-deliver, get the 5★ (per order)** — Respond within hours, deliver early, include the Loom walkthrough even on cheaper tiers at first. A wall of 5★ reviews in month one sets up everything that follows. Then raise prices.

## 08 — Pro Tips

_Edge that most sellers miss_

- **Respond to messages in under 1 hour** — Fiverr ranks gigs partly on response time. Set up the Fiverr app with notifications. Fast replies in the first 24h of an enquiry dramatically lift your conversion and ranking.
- **Always "message before ordering"** — Encourage buyers to message first. It lets you scope properly, avoid mismatched orders (which risk bad reviews), and upsell the right package. Mismatched auto-orders are the #1 cause of disputes.
- **Your demos ARE your portfolio** — Unlike most Fiverr sellers showing static mockups, your live Vercel demos let buyers click around real sites. This is a massive trust signal and almost no one at your price point does it.
- **Funnel premium leads off-platform (legally)** — When a buyer needs something bigger (full Umbraco build, e-commerce, ongoing dev), you can complete it on Fiverr at a custom quote. Repeat clients who trust you become your premium pipeline.
- **Use Buyer Requests early** — New sellers can browse Buyer Requests and send custom offers. In your first weeks, send 5–10 tailored offers per day. This is the fastest way to land that crucial first order before reviews exist.
- **Bank the Fiverr income separately** — Route Fiverr payouts straight to your "Tesla Fund" / "Debt Nuke" pots from your main plan. Keep 20% aside for HMRC. Every clean order moves both goals forward.
