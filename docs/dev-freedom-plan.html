📋 Tax & Legal — What You Need to Know
As a UK resident doing freelance work on the side of employment, you must register as self-employed with HMRC if earnings exceed £1,000/year. Here's what to do:
Register for Self Assessment at gov.uk/register-for-self-assessment — takes 10 minutes online.
Open a free FreeAgent or Wave account to track all income and expenses from day one. Makes tax return trivial.
Save 20% of gross earnings in a separate pot — this covers your income tax and NI on profits above your Personal Allowance.
You can claim home office costs, equipment, software subscriptions, and business mileage as allowable expenses.
Consider forming a Ltd company once monthly earnings exceed £3,000 consistently — more tax efficient, better client credibility.
